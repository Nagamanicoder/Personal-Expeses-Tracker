Getting Started in 6 Simple Steps

Follow this comprehensive guide to master your expense tracking journey

1

Create Your Account

Begin by signing up with your email address and creating a secure password. This creates your personal workspace for expense tracking.

  • Use a valid email address
  • Choose a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
  • Your data is securely stored
2

Set Your Monthly Budget

Enter your total monthly budget amount. This becomes your spending limit and baseline for all calculations and warnings.

  • Use realistic budget values
  • Budget can be updated anytime
  • Basis for balance calculations
3

Record Your Expenses

Add expenses by filling the expense form with complete details. Each entry contributes to your spending analytics.

  • Expense name (e.g., "Groceries", "Fuel")
  • Amount spent in rupees
  • Category (Food, Clothing, Housing, Other)
4

Monitor Your Progress

View all recorded expenses in a comprehensive table. The dashboard automatically calculates your total spending and remaining balance.

  • See total amount spent
  • Check remaining budget
  • View real-time calculations
5

Filter & Manage Entries

Organize your expenses using the built-in category filter. Edit or delete entries as needed to maintain accurate records.

  • Filter by expense category
  • Edit existing entries
  • Delete incorrect entries
6

Stay Informed with Alerts

The system provides intelligent alerts when spending approaches or exceeds budget thresholds, helping you make informed financial decisions.

  • Warning at 80% of budget
  • Large expense alerts (25% of budget)
  • Real-time notifications
